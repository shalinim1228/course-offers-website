OpenCV

OpenCV (Open Source Computer Vision Library) is a powerful tool for real-time computer vision and machine learning. It contains various functionalities to process and analyze images and video, making it essential for applications like face detection, object recognition, and motion tracking.

Developed by Intel and now maintained by the OpenCV Foundation, it is widely used in academic research and commercial applications due to its robust and comprehensive features. OpenCV provides a wide range of algorithms for image processing, computer vision, and machine learning.

The library supports multiple programming languages, including C++, Python, and Java, making it versatile and accessible for developers across different platforms and environments.
